Frequently Asked Questions

How do you determine neighborhood safety scores?
Our neighborhood safety scores are calculated using a comprehensive algorithm that considers multiple factors including crime rates (weighted by type and recency), proximity to emergency services, school safety ratings, community watch programs, street lighting quality, and resident feedback. The data is updated quarterly to ensure accuracy.
How accurate are your school ratings?
Our school ratings incorporate data from state education departments, standardized test scores, graduation rates, teacher-student ratios, parent reviews, and extracurricular opportunities. We partner with educational experts to ensure our methodology prioritizes factors that genuinely impact educational quality and safety.
Do you have information on neighborhoods outside major cities?
Yes! Our database covers neighborhoods in all 50 states, including suburban and rural areas. While data may be more comprehensive in metropolitan regions, we strive to provide detailed safety information for communities of all sizes across the country.
How often is your neighborhood data updated?
We update our neighborhood safety data quarterly, crime statistics monthly, and school information annually (or whenever new official data is released). Property listings and amenities information are updated in real-time as changes occur in our database.
Can I request a safety analysis for a specific neighborhood?
Absolutely! If you don't see your neighborhood of interest in our database, you can request a custom safety analysis. Simply contact our customer service team with the neighborhood details, and we'll prepare a comprehensive report within 3-5 business days.
