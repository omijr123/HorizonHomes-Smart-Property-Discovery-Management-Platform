Our Story

Founded in 2015, HorizonHomes began with a simple vision: to transform the way people find and purchase real estate. Our founders, having experienced firsthand the challenges of the property market, set out to create a platform that would make the process transparent, efficient, and enjoyable.

Today, we've grown to become one of the leading real estate agencies in the region, with a portfolio of over 500 properties and a team of dedicated professionals who are passionate about helping our clients find their perfect home or investment opportunity.
